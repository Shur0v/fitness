Additional Tips & Best Practices

  • Portion Control: Adjust serving sizes to match your personal calorie needs (300–500 kcal below maintenance).
  • Hydration: Aim for 2–3 liters of water daily, more if you sweat heavily.
  • Consistency: Stick to the schedule as closely as possible, but be flexible when needed (e.g., swapping workout days).
  • Progressive Overload: Increase weights, duration, or intensity gradually to avoid plateaus.
  • Recovery: Prioritize sleep (7–9 hours) and active rest to allow muscle repair.
